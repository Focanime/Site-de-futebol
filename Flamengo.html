Club History

Flamengo was founded on November 17, 1895 for disputes
of rowing. The team's entry into football took place in 1912.
Currently, the rubro-negro team is the biggest winner in the history of the
Carioca Championship, with 31 titles. According to several surveys, it is the
club with the largest number of fans in the country.

Flamengo's biggest idol

All titles by Zico for Flamengo:

Already wearing the 10 shirt that he immortalized at the club, he won
the Libertadores da América and the Intercontinental Cup, the Club World Cup,
in 1981, the greatest glory in the team's history. With Flamengo, Zico also
won four Brazilian titles, and six times the Campeonato Carioca.
