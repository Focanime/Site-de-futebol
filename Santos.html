Club History

Santos Futebol Clube was founded on April 14, 1912, on the initiative of three
athletes from the city Francisco Raymundo Marques, Mário Ferraz de Campos and
Argemiro de Souza Júnior who called an assembly at the headquarters of Clube
Concordia to create a football team. During the meeting, the question arose as
to the name that would be given to this association several suggestions appeared
such as, Brasil Atlético, Euterpe and Concordia but the meeting participants
unanimously decided on the proposal by Edmundo Jorge de Araújo: the name Santos
Foot-Ball Club.
