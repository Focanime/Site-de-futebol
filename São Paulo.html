Club History

The São Paulo emerged through the merger of two old clubs:
the Paulistano, great champion at the beginning of the 20th century and the
Association Atlética das Palmeiras, which joined in 1930 giving
origin to "Clube Athlético São Paulo" or "São Paulo da Floresta", as
many called him.
